FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What does the AI video analyzer actually detect? +
It detects editing style, pacing, transition types, caption style, motion graphics presence, color grade look, camera style (handheld, cinematic, etc.), effects, and sound design approach. It then auto-fills your project form with everything it found — saving you and your client the back-and-forth.
Which payment methods does EditFlow support? +
Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal, Wise, and bank transfer. You can collect advance payments, milestone payments, or full payment at the end. All invoices are generated automatically and sent to the client.
Are the AI contracts legally valid? +
AI-generated contracts are customizable templates used as a starting point. They include standard freelance and agency clauses covering payment terms, IP ownership, revision limits, and delivery. We always recommend reviewing with a legal professional for high-value engagements. Digital signatures use DocuSign-compatible standards and are legally binding in 150+ countries.
Can I use EditFlow for my whole team? +
Yes. Studio plan includes up to 5 team members with role-based access (admin, editor, motion designer, colorist, sound designer, manager). Agency plan supports unlimited seats. You can assign tasks, track individual performance, and collaborate on projects in real time.
What happens to my data and client files? +
All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Files are stored on AWS S3 with optional Cloudinary CDN. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Automatic backups run daily. Your data is never used to train AI models or shared with third parties.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes — all plans include a 14-day free trial with full feature access, no credit card required. You can import your existing projects and client list during the trial to see exactly how EditFlow fits your workflow.
